Introduction

Use built-in software package of python called pygame to develop games. pygame contains many built-in modeling groups to make it easier for us to write programs. For example, in the game, I use “pygame.sprite.Sprite”, which is a helper in pygame module written for a role, I just need to create a role to inherit this class, just like put in a formula. In the game, I created three categories: Player, Bullet and Rock. The speed and position are randomly generated after importing random function. If the Rock collides with the Bullet, the point will be added. If the player is hit by the Rock, the life in the upper left corner will be reduced by 10% at a time until the life is exhausted.
