Precision improvement



method one:
data augmentation

Rotating, reflecting, mirroring, flipping or changing the brightness and color temperature of the image can create a larger and more complete amount of data, simulate different situations, and make the application of the model more perfect.As shown on the left.




method two:
test different YOLO version and compare it
(YOLO4-YOLO7)

From YOLOv4, YOLOv5, YOLOv6, and YOLOv7, select the most accurate method among various models. After downloading the official YOLOv4, YOLOv5, YOLOv6, and YOLOv7 built-in models for testing, we found that the test result of YOLOv7 (general objects such as cats and dogs) is better. However, after we trained with custom training weights (chest pain expression), the detection results of YOLOv7 were poor, but the training results of YOLOv4 and YOLOv6 were the best, so we finally chose YOLOv4 and YOLOv6 as the basis for training.As shown on the right.


Operating occasion

Set up the system in the actual medical field. When the patient goes to the hospital for medical treatment, let the doctor diagnose other patients, or when there are affairs that need to be dealt with immediately, the identification system we built can be used for judgment to reduce the delay time and reduce damage.
